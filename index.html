Get to know me

Hello.

Thank you for taking the time to come and visit my website and get to know a little bit about me.

My name is Julian Goodman - I am a Front End Web Developer.

My love for coding / programming began in 2018 when I suddenly thought to myself "I wonder what coding look's like?" - and now I'm here, coding for real and eagerly going after a career in web development.

Now, I've always loved technology and design, which is why when I left school, I headed in the direction of a Design Degree.

However, design alone didn't really cut it as it were... there was something missing...

Whilst on this journey of self discovery, I had worked in retail from a sales assisant, up to the heights of being part of the management team at Nike.

This has given me great transferable skills, such as listening and communication, team-work, time management, critical thinking and problem solving to name a few.

I am super excited to see what the future holds and to fully utilise the skills I have had the chance to develop over the past decade.
